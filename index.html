September 19, 2025

In a Recent publication, We Uncover Critical Insights into Motorcycle Safety in Africa

Our latest paper, "Seeing the Threat: How Drivers React to Motorcycles in African Traffic," has been accepted by the prestigious IEEE Intelligent Transportation System Society Conference. This pioneering study sheds light on crucial driver behaviors, offering vital data to improve road safety and policy in the region.

Our Research

Our research interests span Generative AI, Business Optimization, Entrepreneurial Ecosystems, Edge Computing, Mobility Analytics, and Intelligent Transportation Systems.

Pioneering Digital Twins and Mobility

Adapting traffic simulation for Sub-Saharan African cities, integrating Informal Public Transport (IPT) dynamics to optimize transit efficiency and urban mobility planning.

Federated Computing and Graph Theory

Applying graph theory principles to build scalable and robust federated computing solutions. Exploring data privacy while training powerful AI models.

Gen AI and Applications

This project explores the transformative capabilities of Generative AI across diverse domains, from agentic deployment patterns to complex problem-solving.
